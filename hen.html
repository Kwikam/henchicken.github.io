Welcome to Hen & Chicken

Hen & Chicken is a cozy and vibrant restaurant located in the heart of Horsham, West Sussex. Known for its warm ambiance and delicious cuisine, we offer a unique dining experience that combines classic flavors with modern twists. Whether you're here for our signature cocktails, mouth-watering specials, or lively events, we promise good times and great memories.
